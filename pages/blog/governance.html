PAXI Governance: Voting & Proposals

On PAXI, governance proposals are published to the chain for community review. Validators and delegators can participate in voting. Common proposal types include parameter changes, software upgrades, and community fund spending.

How to Stay Informed

  • Follow official PAXI social channels for proposal announcements.
  • Use PingPub or compatible explorers to monitor proposal status and timelines.
  • Review validator votes, rationales, and community discussions.
