Delegating to NervCore on PAXI

  1. Open PingPub staking page for NervCore (link below).
  2. Connect your wallet (e.g., Keplr) and approve access.
  3. Select the amount to delegate, review commission (15%), and confirm.
  4. Wait for confirmation; your delegation will start earning rewards.
